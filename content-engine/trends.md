## What's hot in the AI-parenting conversation right now
- **"ChatGPT as co-parent" virals** — a mom's TikTok on AI as emotional/logistical
  support hit 710K+ views (meal plans, aisle-sorted lists, gift finding, packing
  lists). The comments call it a "co-parent / therapist / doula." We live here —
  lean in, but keep our take grounded: AI carries the *list*, you carry the *kid*.
- **Mental-load framing wins** — coverage keeps citing that partnered moms do 2×+ the
  cognitive labor. Captions that name the mental load outperform generic "hack" framing.
- **The "analog summer" counter-trend** — slow childhood, boredom, backyard play, fewer
  scheduled activities. Don't fight it; flip it: *AI plans the screen-free day so the
  kids can be bored on purpose.* **(Used: 6/17 carousel)**
- **Seasonal beats ahead:** Father's Day (Sun 6/21), July 4th travel (road-trip prompts
  week of 6/22), back-to-school anxiety content starts mid-July.
