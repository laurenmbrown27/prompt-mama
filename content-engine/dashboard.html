The best candidate rarely wins. The clearest resume does.

After 15+ years in tech enablement (and a lot of hiring panels), here are the 4 AI prompts I give anyone rewriting their resume:

1. Mine the wins: "Interview me one question at a time about my last role until you have 10 accomplishments with numbers. Don't let me be modest."

2. Match the job: "Here's the job description and my resume. List the gaps, the keyword misses, and what to reorder."

3. Rewrite the bullets: "Verb-first, one line each, leading with the result — not the task."

4. The recruiter test: "You're a recruiter with 30 seconds. Skim this and tell me what you remember. What got lost?"

Prompt #1 matters most for women and parents re-entering the workforce — we under-claim our own wins, and an AI interviewer doesn't let us.
