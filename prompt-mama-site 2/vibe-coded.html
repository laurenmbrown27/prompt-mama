It started the way most of my projects do — a problem I couldn't stop poking at, a notebook of prompts, and a husband willing to listen at the kitchen table. I kept asking: why is the hardest season of life the one with the least support?

So we vibe coded. We prototyped. We talked to caregivers. We talked to engineers. We kept going until the prototype turned into a platform, and the platform turned into a team.
