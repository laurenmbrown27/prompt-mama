Honest answers

I'm really not techy. Is this still for me?

Especially for you. Every step is written like I'm sitting next to you on the couch. If you can copy and paste, you can do this.

Do I need to pay for the AI tools?

Weeks 1–3 work great on free versions. Week 4's hands-off finale uses Claude Cowork, which needs a paid Claude plan — but I also show you a free way to get the same result, so you're never stuck.

Is my information safe?

You're in control. The quiz asks how much you want to share, and your plan adapts to it — including a "keep everything private, connect nothing" path. Every day follows the same golden rules: no passwords or sensitive numbers, and a human on every send.

What's "Cowork"?

It's the difference between asking AI a question and handing it a job. By Week 4 you'll give an assistant a multi-step task — and come back to it done.

How long do I have access?

Forever. Buy once, keep your plan, repeat it, or run the other track later. After purchase you can also save your plan as a PDF.
